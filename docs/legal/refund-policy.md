# Refund and Cancellation Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026  
**Version:** 1.0-draft

> **Draft for counsel review.**

## Subscriptions

- Kloqra subscriptions are billed in advance (monthly or annually via Stripe).
- **Trials:** 30-day trials do not require payment. Cancel before trial end to avoid charges.
- **Cancellation:** Organization owners may cancel at any time via **Account → Billing → Manage billing** (Stripe Customer Portal). Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period unless otherwise stated at checkout.
- **No partial refunds:** We do not provide prorated refunds for unused time in a billing period, except where required by applicable law.
- **Charge disputes:** Contact billing@kloqra.com within 14 days of a charge if you believe it was made in error.

## Downgrades

Downgrading to a lower plan takes effect at the next renewal. Workspace and seat limits of the new plan apply immediately if current usage exceeds limits.

## Suspension for non-payment

If payment fails, the subscription may enter `past_due` status. New time entries may be blocked until payment is updated. Data remains accessible for export.

## Enterprise

Custom agreements may specify different refund terms in the order form.
