# Data Processing Agreement (Template)

**Version:** 1.0-draft  
**Last updated:** June 2026

> **Template for enterprise customers.** Counsel must review before execution.

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") forms part of the agreement between **Customer** (controller) and **Kloqra** (processor) for the Kloqra Service.

## 1. Subject matter

Processor processes personal data on behalf of Customer as described in the Terms of Service and this DPA, solely to provide the Service.

## 2. Categories of data and subjects

- **Subjects:** Customer's employees, contractors, and other workspace users.
- **Categories:** identification data, professional activity data (time entries, project assignments), and technical logs necessary for the Service.

## 3. Processor obligations

Processor shall:

- Process personal data only on documented instructions from Customer.
- Ensure personnel are bound by confidentiality.
- Implement appropriate technical and organizational measures (see Annex II).
- Assist Customer with data subject requests where feasible.
- Notify Customer without undue delay of a personal data breach.
- Delete or return personal data upon termination, subject to legal retention requirements.

## 4. Subprocessors

Customer authorizes Processor to engage subprocessors listed in [subprocessors.md](./subprocessors.md). Processor will provide notice of material changes and an opportunity to object.

## 5. Audits

Upon reasonable request, Processor will provide information necessary to demonstrate compliance and allow audits no more than once per year with 30 days' notice.

## 6. International transfers

Transfers outside the EEA/UK use Standard Contractual Clauses or other approved mechanisms.

## Annex I — Processing details

| Item     | Detail                                   |
| -------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| Duration | Term of the subscription agreement       |
| Nature   | Hosting, storage, backup, support        |
| Purpose  | B2B time tracking and project management |

## Annex II — Security measures

Reference: [SECURITY.md](../development/SECURITY.md) — tenant isolation, RBAC, encryption in transit, audit logging.
