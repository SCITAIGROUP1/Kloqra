# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026  
**Version:** 1.0-draft

> **Draft for counsel review.** Do not publish or link in production until `SIGNOFF.md` is complete.

## 1. Who we are

Kloqra provides a B2B SaaS platform for time tracking and project management. This policy describes how we collect, use, and share personal data when you use our Service.

## 2. Roles

- **Organization customers** are data controllers for member and project data they enter in workspaces.
- **Kloqra** is a data processor for workspace content and a controller for account, billing, and platform operations data.

## 3. Data we collect

| Category     | Examples                                 | Purpose                         |
| ------------ | ---------------------------------------- | ------------------------------- |
| Account      | Name, email, password hash               | Authentication, support         |
| Organization | Company name, slug, subscription status  | Multi-tenant account management |
| Usage        | Time entries, projects, tasks            | Core product functionality      |
| Billing      | Stripe customer ID, plan, payment status | Subscription management         |
| Technical    | IP address, user agent, audit logs       | Security, fraud prevention      |

## 4. Legal bases (EEA/UK)

We process data on bases including contract performance, legitimate interests (security, product improvement), and consent where required (e.g. marketing cookies if used).

## 5. Sharing and subprocessors

We use subprocessors listed in [subprocessors.md](./subprocessors.md). We do not sell personal data.

## 6. International transfers

Data may be processed in the United States and other countries where subprocessors operate. Appropriate safeguards (e.g. SCCs) apply for EEA/UK transfers.

## 7. Retention

- Active account data: retained while the subscription is active.
- Churned organizations: retained for a configurable period (default 30 days) then permanently deleted after export opportunity.
- Audit and billing records: retained as required by law.

## 8. Your rights

Depending on jurisdiction, individuals may have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, port, or object to processing. Organization owners may request a **tenant data export** from Account settings. Contact privacy@kloqra.com for requests we cannot fulfill in-product.

## 9. Security

We implement access controls, encryption in transit, tenant isolation, and regular security reviews. See [SECURITY.md](../development/SECURITY.md).

## 10. Children

The Service is not directed at children under 16.

## 11. Changes

We will post material changes here and update the "Last updated" date.
